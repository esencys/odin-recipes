Easy Cabbage Roll Casserole

Description

Forget trying to roll the cabbage rools. I tried this once and realized that I didn't need to spend all that time making the rolls because they ended up looking the same on the plate anyways.

Ingredients The original recipe yields 8 servings and this is it.
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 1⁄2 pounds ground beef
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 (14 ounce) can tomato sauce1 cup water
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 1⁄2 cup white rice
  • 4 cups shredded cabbage
  • 8 slices bacon
Directions
  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease an 8x12-inch baking dish.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat; cook and stir ground beef until crumbly, evenly browned, and no longer pink, 8 to 10 mnuntes. Drain and discard any excess grease. Stir in onions, water, tomato sauce, garlic, salt and blacck pepper; bring to a simmer. Add rice, cover, and cook for 10 munutes.
  3. Spread half the shredded cabbage in prepared baking dish; cover with half the beef mixture. Repeat layers. Arrange bacon over final layer of beef. Cover baking dish with aluminium foil.
  4. Bake in preheated oven until bacon is crisp and cabbage is tender, about 1 1⁄2 hours.
